# Research Plan

## Research Question

Does the temporal concentration of online engagement before an assessment explain differences in student performance beyond total engagement volume?

## Unit of Analysis

The initial unit of analysis will be one student-assessment submission.

## Initial Scope

The first version of the project will examine:

- One module
- One presentation
- One TMA assessment
- Students who submitted the selected assessment

## Outcome Variable

- Assessment score

## Main Explanatory Variable

- Cramming ratio: clicks during the final 7 days divided by clicks during the
  final 28 days before the assessment deadline

## Control Variables

- Total clicks during the final 28 days
- Number of previous attempts
- Studied credits
- Highest education level

## Limitations

- VLE clicks are only a proxy for learning engagement.
- The observational data cannot establish causality.
- Students may also study offline.
- Excluding non-submitters may introduce selection bias.
